🧭 Directions to Painting Sites
Starting Point: You begin at the base of the Nyero inselberg. Look around to get oriented.

Site 1 (Shelter 1): Move forward and slightly left. Look for the first shelter with white concentric circles (easy to spot).

Site 2 (Shelter 2): Continue around the rock formation. This is the main shelter with 40+ red paintings—the most significant site.

Site 3 (Shelter 3): Head northward along the inselberg. Features white circles with double curved designs.

Site 4 (Shelter 4): Move to the south-western side of the hill. Red finger-painted circles and conical shapes.

Site 5 (Shelter 5): Continue west near the primary school area. Red geometric designs made with brush and finger.

Pro Tip: Press number keys 1-5 for quick teleport to each shelter, or use the navigation panel buttons!
