#Project Title:   
DEVELOPMENT OF AN AVR-BASED VIRTUAL TOUR AND MANAGEMENT PROTOTYPE FOR NYERO ROCK PAINTING

## Problem and solution:

Current preservation and tourism rely on static observation and traditional oral guiding.
The current negative situation can be categorized into three main pillars: environmental degradation, destructive human activities, and the limitations of physical tourism management.

## How the system solve the problem.
Remote access to Nyero, enriched on-site experience with AR
Digital documentation and annotation capabilities for future study.
